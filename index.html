Introduction


Grupo 3S Aceros Venezuela

Welcomes the international and Venezuelan market, a variety of wire products. As a result of our quality ensure tight control and continuous insurance over all our raw materials, process and finished products that comply to international standards and specifications of our customers. To achieve this quality, the plant has wire making, galvanizing and wire drawing equipment which are fully automated. It is in the Zona Industrial Chirica de San Felix, Estado Bolivar, on 10.000 m² area and 4.800 m² covered area. This catalogue shows the essential features of new products, however the company can develop other products not shown in this catalogue, to meet the customers’ requirements.
